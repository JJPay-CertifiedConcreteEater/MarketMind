PRIVACY POLICY
Last updated: March 5th, 2026

DATA WE COLLECT
The bot collects certain data to improve server moderation and anything else it may be needed for, such as:
* USER ID'S AND USERNAMES: To identify users for logs and AI interactions.
* MESSAGE CONTENT: To process AI commands and provide "Message Edit/Delete" logging features.
* SERVER METADATA: Server IDs are used to store local configuration and settings.

HOW DATA IS USED
* AI FUNCTIONALITY: When you interact with the AI, your message is sent to Groq for processing. This content is also logged temporarily to make interations more efficient.
* LOGGING: Deleted and edited messages are stored temporarily in the server's designated log channel. This is also the same for profile changes.
* IMPROVEMENT: We do not use your data to "train" our own models or sell your data to third parties.

DATA RETENTION
* TRANSIENT DATA: Messages sent to the AI are not stored permanently on our servers.
* LOGGED DATA: Information in log channels is stored within Discord. MarketMind does not maintain a separate database of your messages unless explicitly stated for a specific feature (e.g., a "notes" command).

THIRD-PARTY SERVICES
We utilize the following third-party services:
* DISCORD API: For bot functionality and message delivery.
* GROQ API: For generative AI responses. Please refer to Groq’s privacy policy regarding their data handling.

YOUR RIGHTS
You have the right to:
* Request the deletion of any stored data associated with your User ID. However, the owners/developers of the bot have the right to say no if there is no probable cause.
* Restrict the bot's access by updating channel permissions or removing the bot from your server. The owners/developers of the bot have the right to say no if there is no probable cause with this as well.

For any questions or data deletion requests, please contact the developer at: @concrete_taste_good_fr (Discord Username).
